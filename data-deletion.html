User Data Deletion Policy – Life Partner

Effective Date: 30 August 2025

At Life Partner, we respect your privacy. You have full control over your personal data and may request deletion at any time.

How to Delete Your Account and Data

Option 1: Delete directly from the App

  1. Open the Life Partner App.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, request deletion by contacting:

Email: winshin.dev@gmail.com

Please include: your full name, the email linked to your Facebook or Google account, and a statement requesting deletion.

We will process your request and confirm once deletion is completed.

Important Note: Uninstalling the app alone will not delete your account or data. You must follow one of the methods above.
