Privacy Policy – Life Partner

Effective Date: 30 August 2025

Life Partner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Life Partner application (“App”) and related services.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, gender, date of birth, photos, preferences, and other profile details.
  • Content: Messages, likes, matches, and other interactions within the app.

b) Information from Third Parties (Login Providers)

  • Facebook Login: Public profile (name, age, gender, photo) and email address.
  • Google Login: Name, profile picture, and email address associated with your Google account.

c) Device and Usage Information

  • Device ID, IP address, operating system, app version.
  • Location data (if you grant permission), to show nearby matches.
  • Activity data: interactions, clicks, preferences, and time spent in the app.

d) Payment Information

If you purchase premium features, we may collect payment-related data through third-party providers. We do not store your full credit card or banking information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your Life Partner account.
  • Help you find and connect with potential matches.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Send updates, notifications, and promotional content (if you opt-in).
  • Monitor usage, prevent fraud, and ensure safety.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

  • With Other Users: Profile information visible according to your settings.
  • With Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, support, payment processing.
  • For Legal Reasons: Compliance with law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • In Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

4. Data Retention

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: personal information removed within 30 days unless legally required.
  • Aggregated anonymized data may be retained for analytics.

5. Data Deletion

You may delete your account at any time:

  • In the App: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  • By Email: winshin.dev@gmail.com

Once deleted, your account and personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days, except where legally required.

6. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

  • Access personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct or update information.
  • Delete account and associated data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., location or marketing).
  • Request a copy of your data in portable format.

Contact: winshin.dev@gmail.com

Your data may be transferred to servers in other countries. By using the app, you consent to such transfers.
